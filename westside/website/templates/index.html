WHO IS WESTSIDE?

Westside is a large and diverse group with people from many backgrounds. Most of us are from the Greater Western Sydney region, especially from Macarthur and South Western Sydney. Our diversity and multiculturalism is an aspect of the club which we take pride in.


Our players and coaches have loads of experience playing volleyball on state and national teams in Australia, and even competing on an International level. Joining the family, means being able to connect with people who have a long history with volleyball and knowledge of the game acquired over years of experience.
